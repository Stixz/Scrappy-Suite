## Name: Scrappy Suite

## Purpose: To help me to keep track of any and everything, and provide the apps and tools I need and, allow access to folders and files, via modules.

### In the current project, the idea is that the Writer module and the Markdown module would each retain their current context of the editor section when switched in and out between other modules. The To Writer and To Markdown is suppose to take highlighted text and copy it to the current edit in respectful modules. This design isn't working as intended.

## Other
Across the top (header) are the labels for each module, tool drop down, settings, and standard window icons (min, max, close.)
The screen will be divided into three panels. Each housing an active module. The panel will change modules when you have it selected and click on a different module.
